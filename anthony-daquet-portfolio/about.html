ABOUT

Hi! I'm Anthony. My passion for design started in early high school and I have been practicing it ever since. I've been a professional Graphic Designer for about 6 years. I've worked at various places such as Marvel Entertainment, Canon Solutions America and Minuteman Press. I currently work at Professional Retail Services and its sister companies, Retail Security Services and Retail Mechanical Services. I design for their social media, digital platforms and work on their marketing needs.

Aside from design, I am obsessed with anything superhero related from comics to TV shows and movies. I also enjoy reading manga, playing video games, Dungeons & Dragons and cooking.
